About Us

One person's love for coffee met another person's love of tea and together, they combined their passion for creativity and taste to fuel their dream of owning a cafe. The couple John and Jane started Berry Bean Cafe in 2015 and has become a staple of the community. The inclusion of seasonal treats along with the classic staples has turned any first-time customer into a regular!
