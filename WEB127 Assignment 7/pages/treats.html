Treats

No morning coffee or tea is complete without a small pastry or treat to accompany it. All our pastries are made in house, and our breakfast sandwiches are made to order. Pastries include cookies, muffins, croissants, and breakfast sandwiches. Bagels can be toasted with cream cheese or jams, and can also be made in conjuction with our breakfast sandwiches. We are always bringing new flavors and treats, so there may be something different to try at every visit! This week, we are unveiling our golden brownies - served well with a cup of coffee or our mint mochas! Everyone deserves a treat, stop by and get yours today.
