Tea and Friends

Refreshing and cool iced tea can be right at your fingertips! Our iced tea is the perfect blend of soothing sweetness and natural bitterness that tea lovers are sure to enjoy. We elevated and upgraded some of the classic flavors, and our seasonal flavors always take a new twist to beloved favorites. Tea flavors include lemon, peach, raspberry, mint, and oolong. We also offer both black and green tea varieties for every flavor. Every order can also be made either hot or cold per request. Add a splash of milk to your tea, or an added espresso shot to your chai!
